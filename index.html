About Us

WL Lighting SDN BHD, founded in 2015 and based in Penang, Malaysia, is a dedicated lighting wholesaler supplying high-quality, affordable lighting solutions to retailers across the country. Our mission is simple ‚Äî to make reliable, energy-efficient lighting more accessible to everyone.

We specialize in a wide range of LED lighting products for both indoor and outdoor use. From downlights, eyeball lights, and surface-mounted fixtures for interiors to gate lamps, wall lamps, and bollard lights for outdoor environments, we offer versatile solutions for various lighting needs. All our products are carefully selected to meet industry standards and come with warranty coverage for peace of mind.

At WL Lighting SDN BHD, we focus on building strong relationships with lighting shops throughout Malaysia by offering competitive pricing, consistent product quality, and dependable supply. While we currently operate through a physical store and do not provide installation or customer support services, we stand by the quality and performance of every product we offer.

Our goal is to see WL Lighting SDN BHD products brighten more spaces and reach more people across the country. We may be simple in structure, but we're serious about lighting up the future.
